# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 27, 2026

## 1. Privacy-first design

LedgeIT is designed to keep your financial records private and under your control. Core data such as income, expenses, savings, investments, assets, debts, insurance, goals, and related notes is stored locally on your device.

## 2. Information processed in the app

Depending on how you use LedgeIT, the app may process:

- Financial entries you create (amounts, categories, dates, account/source fields)
- Goals, targets, and progress values
- Report inputs and derived totals used for charts and exports
- Optional metadata such as reminder settings or app preferences

## 3. Imports, exports, and backups

LedgeIT may let you import and export data (for example CSV, PDF, or HTML reports). Files you export are created on your device or to locations you explicitly choose (for example Files/iCloud destinations under your control).

You are responsible for how exported files are stored or shared after export.

## 4. Analytics, diagnostics, and crash reports

Some builds may include optional analytics or diagnostic signals to improve performance, reliability, and product quality. When present, these signals are limited to app usage/technical events and are not intended to include your raw financial ledger contents.

## 5. Third-party services

LedgeIT may rely on platform or third-party services for app distribution, billing, or support features (for example Apple App Store services). Data handled by those providers is governed by their own privacy policies and terms.

## 6. Data sharing and selling

We do not sell your personal financial data. We do not share your identifiable ledger records with third parties except when required to operate requested features, comply with legal obligations, or with your explicit action/consent.

## 7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards intended to protect information processed by the app and website. No method of storage or transmission is guaranteed to be 100% secure.

## 8. Your choices and controls

- You can edit or delete entries directly in the app.
- You can remove local app data by uninstalling the app (subject to device backup behavior).
- You can choose whether to create/share exported files.
- You can review App Store privacy labels for additional platform disclosures.

## 9. Children's privacy

LedgeIT is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through this website.

## 10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

## 11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us through the ledgeit.app@gmail.com.
